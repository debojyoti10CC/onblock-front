## Technical Innovation

### Soulbound Tokens (SBTs):
- Non-transferable NFTs
- Represent verified identity
- Can't be sold or stolen

### Zero-Knowledge Proofs:
- Prove you're verified without revealing data
- Bots never see your name, address, etc.
- Privacy-preserving compliance

### Kill Switch:
- On-chain revocation
- Instant (< 1 second)
- Revokes ALL active rails
- Can't be bypassed

### Smart Contracts:

- Automated fee distribution
- Spending limit enforcement
